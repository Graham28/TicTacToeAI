Tic Tac Zero AI

Tic Tac Zero has been trained playing against itself. It is first trained on 10,000 random games.It is then trained on games with a mixture of random moves and moves that the model predicts are good moves.

As the training progresses, the probability of a move being random is reduced. This results in some overtraining of the model, which means it is possible to beat the model trianed on 60,000 games but not the model trained on 40,000 games.
